What is this?

Pick&Counter is your daily collection of dota 2 picks strategy; a community of players that loves to share and experiment about possible pick combinations of dota 2 heroes.

Pick&Counter believes that Dota is a very complex game with countless of possiblities and excitement. Experimenting on even the worst hero combination might probably the next meta.

How to share, comment, upvote or even challenge a post?

* Share

In its early stage we allow anonymous shares/posts, but somehow as the users increases there is a need for us to somehow give ownership to each share so that we would be able to give back credits to each post so for now we only require steam account for signing in users/contributors.

* Steam Account

We are using Steam OpenId Authentication API in our signin process. Only public data is what we have from you.

* Comments

As of now, anonymous users/guest are free to comment to any posts given.

* Upvoting

Upvoting a post is casting your karma points to that person's post.

* Challenge

Challenging a post is one way of helping early adapter of the game, it will show some set of possible counters for that specific post. Challenges are considered as a post that will soon be countered by a different set of picks.

Are there any guideline in sharing?

As simple as the following:

heroes - your hero picks.

notes - a short note on how to execute the picks combo ingame.
